Paneer Masala Recipe : How to make Paneer Masala


Paneer Masala is a North Indian recipe made using Paneer, tomatoes,onions and variety of spices. This recipe is a delicious side dish recipe and is a great option for occasions like anniversaries and parties.



Ingredients of Paneer Masala


250 gm paneer
1 finely chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
1/4 teaspoon powdered turmeric
1/4 teaspoon garam masala powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup peas
2 chopped tomato
1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
1 tablespoon finely chopped coriander leaves
1/4 teaspoon Red chilli powder
1/2 teaspoon powdered cumin
1 tablespoon Refined oil

Recipe-

How to make Paneer Masala

Step 1
Heat oil in a pan. Add the ginger garlic paste and stir-fry for 30 seconds.

Step 2
Add the onions and saute until golden. Add tomatoes and cook, stirring from time to time, for 2 minutes.

Step 3
Add turmeric powder, cumin powder, red chilli powder, peas, garam masala powder and mix well.

Step 4
Add the paneer cubes.Reduce flame and stir well.

Step 5
Simmer or 2 to 3 minutes. Add coriander leaves and remove from flame.

Step 6
Cover the pan with a lid and keep aside for 10 minutes.

Step 7
Stir well. Serve as a side dish.
