Fish Kosha



Ingredients


500 gm rohu fish
2 medium onion
1 tablespoon garlic paste
1 teaspoon turmeric
1 teaspoon red chilli powder
2 leaf bay leaf
1 tablespoon ginger paste
Salt as required
50 gm yoghurt (curd)

HOW TO MAKE FISH KOSHA

Recipe-


Step 1 : Marinate the fish
Marinate the pieces of the fish in turmeric powder and salt for a few minutes. Then, fry it lightly in mustard oil and keep it aside.

Step 2 : Saute onion and prepare gravy
Heat oil in a pan, fry the chopped onion with bay leaf till the onion becomes golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry with the onion on low flame. Add yogurt and red chili powder to it. Add 2 cups of water to it with a half spoon of turmeric powder.

Step 3 : Cook the fish and serve hot
Add salt according to taste and cook on full flame. When the water starts boiling, add the fried fishes to it and cook it on full flame for 5 minutes. When the gravy becomes thick, turn off the flame. Serve hot with steamed rice.
