Tawa Pulao Recipe : How To Make Tawa Pulao


Pulao is an easy-to-make and most sought after rice recipe as it is satiating as well as delicious. Pulao in India is made with various variations like Matar Pulao, Mix Vegetable Pulao, Keema Pulao and so on. One of these lip smacking pulaos is Tawa Pulao


Ingredients of Tawa Pulao


2 cup boiled rice
1/2 cup peas
2 tomato
3 teaspoon pav bhaji masala
1 teaspoon red chilli powder
1 teaspoon cumin seeds
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
1 tablespoon refined oil
1 potato
1/2 cup carrot
1 capsicum (green pepper)
salt as required
1 teaspoon powdered turmeric
1 onion
1 bunch coriander leaves
1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
1/4 cup water
1 tablespoon butter

Recipe-


How To Make Tawa Pulao

Step 1
Heat oil in a pan. Once hot, add jeera seeds and let them sizzle. To this, add chopped onions and fry until translucent. Add ginger garlic paste and fry till the pungent smell subsides. Add tomatoes and cook till tomatoes turn soft.

Step 2
Add all the spice powders(including salt) along with potato and water. Mix well. Turn your flame to low and cook till oil separates from sides.

Step 3
Add carrots, capsicum and green peas to this mixture. Mix all the ingredients and cook for 2-3 mins. Add cooked rice to this mixture and ensure all the ingredients are combined well.

Step 4
Add chopped coriander. Off the flame and squeeze lime while serving. Team this spicy pulao with any mild or simple raitha.
