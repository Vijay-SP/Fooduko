Salli Boti



Ingredients


4 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 tablespoon ginger paste
500 gm cut into strips mutton
1 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
1 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
1/2 teaspoon sugar
2 teaspoon garam masala powder
3 pinches salt
1 cup chopped onion
1 tablespoon garlic paste
1 teaspoon red chilli powder
1 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
1 cup chopped tomato
2 teaspoon vinegar
1 cup cut into strips potato

Recipe-


Step 1
Heat the oil in a pan and add onions to it. Fry till they turn golden brown.

Step 2
Add ginger-garlic paste, mutton strips, red chilli powder and turmeric powder. Mix it well.

Step 3
Now mix in the roasted coriander powder, cumin powder followed by tomatoes and stir again.

Step 4
Add a little sugar and vinegar. Add garam masala and salt, mix well

Step 5
Reduce the flame and cover till cooked for about 15-20 minutes.

Step 6
Add chopped coriander and give it a final stir.

Step 7
Garnish it with more coriander leaves and top it up with lots of Salli (potato strips).
