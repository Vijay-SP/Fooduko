Chicken Fried Rice
~ Priya Moopanar





Ingredients

  • BONELESS CHICKEN (250gm) {cut into small pieces}.
  • Chopped ginger (15gms).
  • Chopped garlic (50gms).
  • Spring onions.
  • Soya sauce (As per taste required).
  • Tomato sauce (As per taste required).
  • Carrot.
  • Capsicum.
  • Cabbage.
  • Pepper powder.
  • Chopped green chillies (20gms).
  • Scrambled eggs.
  • BOILED Basmati Rice (300gms).


Recipe-


1.Heat Refined oil 2tsp for 5mins.
2.Add chopped garlic, ginger and boneless chicken (small chopped pieces).
3.Saute it until the chicken gets cooked.
4.Then add vegetables - Chopped carrot, Chopped capsicum, Chopped cabbage, Chopped Green chillies, White spring onions mix it well for 3-4mins(Don't over cook the vegetables it should be crunchy).
5.Add boiled Basmati Rice with pepper powder 2 tsp, soya sauce - 1 tsp, Tomato sauce - 1tsp mix it well and cook it for 3-4mins.
6.Finally add green spring onions & scrambled eggs. That's it!!

Your chicken fried rice is ready to serve!!!
