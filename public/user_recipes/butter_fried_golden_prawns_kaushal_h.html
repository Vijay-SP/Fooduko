Butter Fried Golden Prawns
~Kaushal Hote




Ingredients


Prawns : small or medium sized
15- 20 de-shelled, tail on(for presentation).
Binding agents : 3 eggs
Seasoning :
Salt (as per taste)
Pepper (0.5 tbsp)
Oyster sauce (1 tbsp)
Chilli powder (0.5 tbsp)
Chilli sauce (1 tbsp)
Dark soy sauce (0.25 tbsp)
Chopped spices : 0.5 ginger, very finely chopped
5 - 6 garlic cloves, very finely chopped
2 Chillies, cut at an angle
Coriander, chopped finely
Fat : 3 Tbsp oil, preferably olive oil; if not, any oil except mustard is fine.
7 tbsp salted butter

Recipe-


1) Get the pan really nice and hot. Add in the oil and 4 tbsp butter together, so that the butter does not burn. Add in the ginger, garlic and saute for a couple of minutes to get a nice, golden - brown tinge. Add in the prawns and stir fry until 85% cooked. The prawns will sweat, do not worry. Just drain out the "water"; it's great for stocks.
2) Tilt the pan, to get all the prawns on one side (so that we get all the residual prawn flavor for the next step). Add the remaining 3 tbsp or more butter (More fat = more flavor). Add the chilles, saute them for a minute or two. Then add the beaten eggs. Create a scramble with medium/small sized curds and add a pinch of salt and pepper to season it.
3) Once the scrambled eggs are fully cooked, mix in the prawns.
4) Add in all the seasonings; oyster, dark soy, chilli sauces, salt, pepper. Stir - fry for a couple of minutes, mixing everything nicely. Incorporate all the richness of the eggs and the butter in the prawns.
5) Cook everything for a final 1.5/2 minutes and plate. Sprinkle a generous dash of finely chopped coriander for a aesthetic, pretty look(also adds a subtle flavor).
